RESEARCH COLLABORATION AGREEMENT

1. Parties
This Research Collaboration Agreement is made between the University and the external research partner.

2. Confidentiality
Each party agrees to keep confidential information secure and not disclose it to third parties without written consent.

3. Intellectual Property
Any intellectual property created during the project will be discussed and agreed by both parties in writing.

4. Publication
The University may publish research outcomes, subject to a reasonable review period by the external partner.

5. Liability
Each party is responsible for its own acts and omissions under this agreement.

6. Termination
Either party may terminate this agreement by providing written notice.

7. Governing Law
This agreement is governed by the laws of New Zealand.
